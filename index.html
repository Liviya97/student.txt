Overview

Sports are constantly evolving over the years. Each training is varied to enjoy the good use of technical and philosophical techniques. We gathered few sport clubs to illustrate the good usage of how to create opportunities for learning to happen. We try to familiarize all of our locals and tourists with what we do, so we can determine of how to make more people to be more active. As a basketball player once said: "Sports do not build characters, they reveal it" - John Wooden. As a clubmates we want to invite people to one of our many sport clubs, so they can have a better view of how mind and body work together. We as a team will give you the best experience - while playing the sport chosen you will find new friends and have fun, without the feeling of the pressure of your daily routine.

Haarlem

About the city

Haarlem is the capital of the province in North Holland. The eighbourhood attracts with its' significant significant ancient history as well as nowadays attraction, like shops, companies, sports, festivals and museums. The city generate more traffic by its own uniqueness, which is sign moreover as a "cultural".

Businesses

The city vibrant cultural scene and high quality of life gives opportunities to new upcoming businesses and already companies located in this area. The fascinating element about the city is that they share the same goal, which stimulates the entrepreneurs' spirit, with its' own highly-educated professionals. The neighbourhood allows for any type of business to be open, because it builds a lot of attraction by the already invented industries.

Attraction

Museums

Museums like "Teylers" is the oldest museum of the Netherlands. The collection that the museum have bring a lot visitors from in and outside the country. Its' own record of 157,843 visitors in 2016 is continuously growing over the years. For the following reason that it holds big amount of exhibitions and ancient history behind.

Festivals

Every year "Bloemencorso" festival takes place. This is one of the important dates of the city, because it changes its modern site into colorful and natural perspective. The flower parade drives from Noordwijk to Haarlem within one day. The characteristic of celebration brings us back to the middle ages, with the significant decoration which covers everything with flowers.

Sports

The city is home to many different active people, because it gatherers a lot of sport clubs, in which the population of this area have the ability to choose with its' own preference of what sport they want to do (as a sport). The neighbourhood holds several international sport competitions where people are continuously learning of how to build a strong attitude towards the sport they choose. All of our tournaments get people enthusiastic about what they had been training up to now and show what they are physically good at. Our major goal is to show all of our athletes how to believe for what they came for. By showing them that everything is worth fighting when persistence is working.
